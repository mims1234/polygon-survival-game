How to Play Wildlands

Overview

Welcome to Wildlands — a survival exploration game set in an endless procedurally generated world. Gather resources, build structures, survive the cold, craft advanced materials, and establish a thriving empire. Your ultimate goal is to build a Market and achieve the empire victory.

Controls

  • Click a tile to move there and interact.
  • 1 — Hand (gather food)
  • 2 — Axe (chop trees for wood)
  • 3 — Pickaxe (mine rocks for stone)
  • 4 — Bucket (collect water)
  • 5 — Build (opens the build menu)
  • M — Toggle World Map
  • H — Open this Help guide
  • Escape — Close map

Core Resources

There are 7 resources in the game:

  • Wood — Gathered from trees with an Axe.
  • Stone — Mined from rocks with a Pickaxe.
  • Food — Gathered from bushes with your Hand.
  • Water — Collected from water tiles with a Bucket.
  • Planks — Crafted from Wood. Used for advanced buildings.
  • Ingots — Smelted from Stone at a Forge.
  • Coins — Minted at a Market. Used for upgrades and trade.

Tools & Upgrades

Your tools can be upgraded to increase yield, speed, and reduce stamina cost. Upgrades require a Forge (Tier II) or a Blast Furnace (Tier III).

  • Tier I (Base): +1 yield per gather
  • Tier II (Forge): +2 yield, faster, less stamina. Costs wood/stone/water.
  • Tier III (Blast Furnace): +3 yield, very fast, very low stamina. Costs ingots + planks + coins.

Click the "Upgrade Tools" button in the Tools panel when a Forge is available.

Buildings & Upgrades

Every production building can be upgraded once for better output. Select a building tile to see the Upgrade button in the Inspector panel. Upgraded buildings have unique 3D models and stronger effects.

Crafting

Open the Craft panel to convert resources. Some recipes require specific buildings:

  • Planks: 3 Wood → 2 Planks (no building needed)
  • Ingots: 4 Stone + 1 Water → 1 Ingot (needs Forge)
  • Ingots (Efficient): 3 Stone + 1 Water → 1 Ingot (needs Blast Furnace)
  • Coins: 3 Food + 1 Plank → 1 Coin (needs Market)
  • Coins (Bulk): 5 Food + 2 Planks → 3 Coins (needs Trade Hub)

Survival: Stamina, Hunger & Temperature

  • Stamina: Decreases when gathering. Regenerates over time. Low stamina slows you down.
  • Hunger: Constantly drains. If it hits zero, stamina drains rapidly. Food is auto-eaten when hunger drops below 55.
  • Temperature (Temp): Shown in the Survival panel. You slowly get colder when away from a Campfire or Bonfire. Night makes you freeze faster.
  • Freezing to Death: If your cold meter fills completely (1 hour without warmth), you die. You respawn at the center with penalties.

Tips: Build a Campfire early. Upgrade it to a Bonfire for a larger safe zone. Shelter and Cabin reduce hunger drain and boost stamina regen.

Automation & Consumption

Automated buildings produce resources over time, but most require fuel:

  • Farm / Irrigated Farm: Produces Food. Farm consumes 1 Water per cycle. Irrigated Farm is free.
  • Lumber Camp / Sawmill: Produces Wood. Consumes 1 Food per cycle.
  • Quarry / Deep Quarry: Produces Stone. Consumes 1 Wood per cycle.
  • Well / Deep Well: Produces Water. Free to operate.
  • Workshop / Factory: Produces Planks. Consumes Wood.

Storage System

You start with only 10 storage capacity. Build structures to increase it:

  • Campfire: +5
  • Bonfire: +10
  • Shelter: +15
  • Cabin: +25

If storage is full, you cannot gather more resources until you spend or craft them.

Exploration & Regions

The world is made of infinite 20×20 regions. Walk to any edge to travel to the next region.

  • Resources are scarce and respawn very slowly. You must explore to find more.
  • Each region has different biome layouts — some are wood-rich, others stone-rich.
  • First visit to a new region grants +30 XP.
  • Press M to see visited and current regions on the World Map.

Progression & Levels

Gain XP by gathering, crafting, building, exploring, and upgrading. Levels unlock new buildings:

  • Lv 1 — Campfire
  • Lv 2 — Shelter
  • Lv 3 — Farm, Well
  • Lv 4 — Watchtower, Lumber Camp, Quarry
  • Lv 5 — Forge, Workshop
  • Lv 6 — Market

Victory Condition

Build a Market to open trade routes and complete the empire victory. You can keep playing after winning. Upgrade the Market to a Trade Hub for even better trade deals and bulk coin production.

Quick Tips

  • Build a Campfire immediately. The cold will kill you.
  • Build a Well early for passive water — it costs nothing to run.
  • Don't over-gather in one region. Resources take 6–10 minutes to respawn.
  • Upgrade your Shelter to a Cabin for much-needed storage.
  • Coins are valuable — they're required for building upgrades and Tier III tools.
  • The Blast Furnace is a major power spike. Prioritize it after your Market.
  • Use the Map (M) to plan exploration and avoid backtracking.
